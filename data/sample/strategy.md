# Strategy & Risk Notes — SYNTHETIC SAMPLE DATA (not real)

> My personal trading framework. Coldwire uses this to ground its conviction scoring
> and risk commentary in MY rules — not generic advice.

## Objective
Grow NAV through a full crypto cycle while never risking ruin. I would rather miss
a pump than blow up. Capital preservation first, asymmetric upside second.

## Position Sizing Rules
- Max risk per single idea: 2% of NAV (distance to stop sizes the position).
- Single-alt cap: 25% of NAV. Single position hard cap: 45%.
- Total alt exposure cap: 50% of NAV. BTC can exceed this; alts cannot.
- Always keep >= 20% cash for opportunistic buys / margin of safety.

## Conviction Framework (how I score 1–5)
- 5 = thesis + catalyst + favorable structure + within risk rules. Full size.
- 4 = strong thesis, minor timing/structure risk. Near full size.
- 3 = mixed — real thesis but a rule conflict or unclear catalyst. Half size / wait.
- 2 = weak / speculative. Tiny tactical size only.
- 1 = avoid. Sentiment-only or rule-violating.

## Risk Management
- Honor stops mechanically. A "mental stop" not acted on is a real loss in disguise.
- Trim winners that breach the single-position cap — concentration is the silent killer.
- Never average down on a TACTICAL trade (only on long-term sleeves: BTC, LINK).
- During alt euphoria (DOGE leading, funding rates spiking), de-risk into BTC/cash.

## Macro / Regime Read
- BTC dominance is my risk-on/off dial. Rising BTC.D = trim alts. Falling = rotate to alts.
- I do NOT trade on news headlines; I trade levels and my pre-defined invalidations.
- Funding rates persistently > 0.05%/8h = crowded longs = expect a flush.

## Behavioral Guardrails
- No revenge trading after a stop-out. Step away 24h.
- Position I'm emotionally attached to (current: SOL winner) gets EXTRA scrutiny, not less.
- If I broke a rule to enter (current: ARB into unlock), the bias is to exit and reset.
